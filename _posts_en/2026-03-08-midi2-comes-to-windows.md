## 2026: The Year MIDI 2.0 Goes Mainstream

2026 marks a major turning point for MIDI 2.0 adoption.

### Windows 11 Gets Native MIDI 2.0 Support

In February 2026, Microsoft released Windows MIDI Services, bringing OS-level MIDI 2.0 support to Windows 11.

Key capabilities include:

- **Native MIDI 2.0 Support**: UMP (Universal MIDI Packet) protocol handled at the OS level
- **Automatic MIDI 1.0/2.0 Translation**: Legacy and MIDI 2.0 devices interoperate seamlessly
- **Multi-Client Access**: Multiple apps access the same MIDI device simultaneously
- **Loopback/App-to-App Routing**: Direct MIDI signal routing between applications
- **USB MIDI 2.0 Class Driver**: Zero-driver recognition of USB MIDI 2.0 devices

### Industry-Wide Momentum

- **MIDI Association 2026 Roadmap**: MIDI 2.0 Property Exchange becomes essential for AI controllers and biometric integration
- **CES 2026**: New MIDI 2.0 compatible gear announced in volume
- **Controller Evolution**: AI sensors and MIDI 2.0 combine to create "intent-predicting" controllers
