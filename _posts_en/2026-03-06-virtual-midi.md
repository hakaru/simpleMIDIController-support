# Connect Apps with Virtual MIDI

No external hardware? No problem. SimpleMidiController's **Virtual MIDI** feature lets you send MIDI signals to other apps on the same iPhone or iPad.

## Use Cases

- Control DAW apps like **GarageBand** or **AUM**
- Manipulate synthesizer app parameters in real time
- Adjust effect app settings on the fly

Build a complete MIDI workflow on a single device — no external hardware required.

## How to Set Up

1. Enable **Virtual MIDI** in SimpleMidiController's settings
2. Open your target app (DAW, synth, etc.)
3. Select "**SimpleMidiController**" as MIDI input in that app

That's all it takes. Buttons and sliders in SimpleMidiController will control the target app in real time.

## Examples

**With GarageBand**: Set SimpleMidiController as GarageBand's MIDI input, and use on-screen pads and sliders to play and control GarageBand instruments.

**Live Performance**: Use SimpleMidiController as a unified control surface across multiple apps in your live setup.

## Works Alongside Other Connections

Virtual MIDI works simultaneously with Bluetooth and USB MIDI. For example, control an external synth via Bluetooth while also sending MIDI to a DAW on your iPad via Virtual MIDI.
