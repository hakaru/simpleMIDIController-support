# Rock-Solid Connection with USB MIDI

When you need the lowest possible latency, **USB MIDI** is the way to go. SimpleMidiController fully supports USB-connected MIDI devices.

## Connection Options

- **USB-C**: Connect directly to newer iPhones and iPads
- **Lightning to USB Camera Adapter**: For Lightning-equipped devices

USB MIDI devices are automatically detected when connected. No drivers or complicated setup needed.

## Bluetooth vs USB MIDI

| | Bluetooth MIDI | USB MIDI |
|---|---|---|
| **Latency** | Low | Lowest |
| **Cables** | None | Required |
| **Stability** | Good | Excellent |
| **Power** | Not needed | Possible via adapter |

Use USB MIDI for recording and precise timing. Use Bluetooth MIDI for live performance and freedom of movement.

## Plug & Play

Connect your USB MIDI device and it's automatically recognized. Unplug and it disconnects cleanly. Simple and reliable.
