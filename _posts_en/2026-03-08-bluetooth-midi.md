# Wireless Freedom with Bluetooth MIDI

One of SimpleMidiController's best features is **Bluetooth MIDI** — wireless connection with no cables or adapters needed. Just pair and play.

## Dead Simple Setup

1. Put your MIDI device in Bluetooth MIDI pairing mode
2. Open SimpleMidiController
3. The app automatically detects available devices
4. Tap to connect

That's it. No complicated configuration required.

## Perfect for Live Performance

Bluetooth MIDI eliminates cable clutter on stage. Keep your devices within 1-2 meters for low-latency, stable connection.

## Auto-Reconnect

Enable "Auto Connect" in settings, and the app will automatically reconnect to your last used device on launch. No need to pair again every time.

## Troubleshooting

- Toggle Bluetooth OFF and ON on your iPhone/iPad
- Restart your MIDI device
- Make sure no other apps are connected to the same device
- Move away from Wi-Fi routers and other interference sources

Experience cable-free performance with Bluetooth MIDI.
