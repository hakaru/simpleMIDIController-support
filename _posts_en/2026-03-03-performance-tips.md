# Tips for the Best Performance

Here are some tips to get the most out of SimpleMidiController.

## When Using Bluetooth MIDI

### Keep Devices Close

Bluetooth MIDI works best within 1-2 meters. Greater distances can increase latency or cause connection instability.

### Avoid Wireless Interference

Wi-Fi routers, microwaves, and USB 3.0 hubs are common 2.4GHz interference sources. Move away from them for a more stable connection.

### Check Battery Levels

Low battery on your iPhone/iPad or MIDI device can degrade Bluetooth performance.

## General Tips

### Close Unused Apps

Too many background apps can drain system resources and cause instability. Close unnecessary apps before live performances.

### Use USB MIDI for Lowest Latency

For recording or any situation where every millisecond counts, USB MIDI provides the lowest latency.

### Verify Bluetooth Permissions

If the app isn't working properly, check iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Bluetooth and make sure SimpleMidiController has permission enabled.
